You configure seasons, create collections, and link them to suppliers. These assignments simplify article creation and analytics reporting.

---

## Prerequisites

- Access to the **Admin App** with **Admin rights**
- Permission for the **Settings** section

---

## 1. Configure seasons

Seasons are created automatically by the system based on your configuration settings.

1. Open the **Admin App**.
2. Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
3. Click **Seasons**.
4. Click **Config Season**.

![Seasons list with configuration button](/images/en/saisons-liste.png)

5. Define:
   - From which **year** seasons should be created automatically
   - Which **month** starts the first semester
   - **Short names** for both semesters
   - How long seasons remain visible in the selection
6. Click **Save**.

The system creates new seasons automatically and hides expired ones.

> **Note:** To remove the season from a label, open the season and enter a space in the **Print Text** field.

---

## 2. Create collections

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Collections**.
3. Click **Create Collection**.

![Collections list with create button](/images/en/kollektionen-liste.png)

4. Fill in the fields:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Name** | Name of the collection. |
| **Print Text** | Text displayed on the label. |
| **Start Month** | Start of the delivery period. |
| **End Month** | End of the delivery period. |

5. Click **Save**.

---

## 3. Link collection to supplier

1. Open the desired collection.
2. Switch to the **Suppliers** tab.
3. Add the suppliers that deliver this collection.

When creating articles and orders, the system automatically suggests the matching collection based on the supplier and delivery period.

---

## Season types

The system distinguishes three season types:

| Type | Description | Usage |
|------|-------------|-------|
| **Article Season** | The season assigned to an article. | Article management. Not recommended for Analytics filters. |
| **Order Season** | The season assigned to an order. | Analytics reporting in the goods flow area. |
| **Receiving Season** | Automatically assigned based on the delivery date. | Automatic assignment when no order exists. |

> **Note:** For Analytics reporting, use the **Order Season** or **Receiving Season**, as the Article Season may be incomplete.

---

## Frequently asked questions

**Are seasons created automatically?**
Yes. The system creates seasons automatically based on your configuration.

**Can I edit collections afterwards?**
Yes. Open the collection and adjust the fields.
