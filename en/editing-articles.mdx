You edit prices, upload images, and adjust article properties — individually or for multiple variants at once.

---

## Prerequisites

- Access to the **Admin App**
- Permission for the **Articles** section
- At least one article with variants exists in the system

---

## Overview

Three common editing tasks:

1. **Change prices** — You adjust purchase price, sales price, or original price.
2. **Upload images** — You add product photos to the article.
3. **Set an original price** — You enter a different original price that appears struck through on the label.

---

## 1. Change prices

### Edit a single variant

1. Open the **Admin App**.
2. Click **Articles** in the sidebar.
3. Open the desired article.
4. Switch to the **Articles** tab.

![Variant list with price columns](/images/en/artikel-varianten-liste.png)

5. Click **Edit** at the end of the row.

The system opens an edit window with two sections: **Attributes** and **Prices**.

![Edit modal with price fields](/images/en/artikel-bearbeiten-modal.png)

6. Adjust the desired price fields:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Purchase Price** | Price at which you buy the article from the supplier. |
| **Sales Price** | Price the customer pays. |
| **MSRP equals SP** | When enabled, the system uses the sales price as MSRP. |
| **MSRP** | Manufacturer's suggested retail price. Only visible when the checkbox is disabled. |

7. Click **Save**.

### Edit multiple variants at once

1. Enable the **checkboxes** next to the desired variants.
2. Click **Edit** in the action bar above the list.
3. Adjust the prices. Changes apply to all selected variants.
4. Click **Save**.

> **Note:** Bulk editing only supports price changes. For attribute changes (color, material, GTIN), edit each variant individually.

---

## 2. Upload images

1. Open the desired article.
2. Switch to the **Overview** tab.
3. Open the **Images** section.

![Overview tab with images section](/images/en/artikel-bilder-upload.png)

4. Drag an image into the upload area or click to select a file.
5. The system uploads the image and displays it in the overview.

> **Note:** You can upload multiple images at once. Use the **Sort** button to change the image order.

---

## 3. Set an original price

When the sales price differs from an original price (e.g., for promotions), the original price appears struck through on the label.

### Single variant

1. Open the article and switch to the **Articles** tab.
2. Click **Edit** at the end of the row.
3. Disable the **SP equals Original Price** checkbox.
4. Enter the **Sales Price** (price the customer pays).
5. Enter the **Original Price** (displayed struck through on the label).
6. Click **Save**.

The system calculates the **discount** automatically as a percentage.

### Multiple variants at once

1. Enable the checkboxes next to the desired variants.
2. Click **Edit**.
3. Disable the **SP equals Original Price** checkbox.
4. Enter the prices.
5. Click **Save**.

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I change the price for all variants at once?**
Yes. Enable the checkboxes next to the desired variants and click **Edit**. The price change applies to all selected variants.

**Where do I upload images?**
Open the article and switch to the **Overview** tab. In the **Images** section, you can upload images via drag-and-drop or the upload dialog.

**What is the difference between sales price and original price?**
The **sales price** is the current price the customer pays. The **original price** appears struck through on the label and shows the previous or regular price.
