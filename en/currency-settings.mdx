## Prerequisites

- Access to the **Admin App** with **Admin rights**
- Permission for the **Settings** section

---

## 1. Purchase settings

Purchase settings define the exchange rate for purchase prices in foreign currency.

1. Open the **Admin App**.
2. Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
3. Click **Purchase Settings**.
4. Click **Add Purchase Setting**.

![Purchase settings](/images/en/einkaufseinstellungen.png)

5. Select a **start date** (must be in the future).
6. Optionally select an **end date**.
7. Select the **currency**.
8. Enter the desired **exchange rate**.
9. Click **Add**.

---

## 2. Sales settings

Sales settings define exchange rates and optional markups for sales in foreign currency.

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
2. Click **Sales Settings**.
3. Click **Add Sales Setting**.

![Sales settings](/images/en/verkaufseinstellungen.png)

4. Optionally enter a **fixed exchange rate**.
5. Optionally enter a **markup** (added to the exchange rate).
6. Select the **currency**.
7. Click **Add**.

> **Note:** The markup can be defined independently from the fixed exchange rate.

---

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I create purchase settings retroactively?**
No. The start date must be in the future.

**Can purchase and sales settings have an end date?**
Yes. Both setting types support an optional end date.
