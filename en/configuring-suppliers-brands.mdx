You configure suppliers and brands, link them together, and set up delivery and payment terms. This configuration is a prerequisite for article creation and order placement.

---

## Prerequisites

- Access to the **Admin App** with **Admin rights** (not possible with a Team Account)
- Permission for the **Settings** section

---

## Overview

1. **Configure a supplier** — You add a supplier with address and currency.
2. **Configure a brand** — You add a brand.
3. **Link brand to supplier** — You define which supplier delivers which brand and configure pricing factors.
4. **Set delivery and payment terms** — You configure delivery conditions, payment deadlines, and early payment discounts.
5. **Invite a contact** — You invite a brand representative via email to send orders directly.

---

## 1. Configure a supplier

1. Open the **Admin App**.
2. Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
3. Click **My Suppliers**.
4. Click **Configure Supplier** in the top right.

![Supplier list with configuration button](/images/en/lieferanten-liste.png)

5. Search for the desired supplier.
6. Select the appropriate **order address** and **currency**.
7. Click **Save**.

![Configure supplier](/images/en/lieferant-konfigurieren.png)

> **Note:** If the supplier does not appear in the search, they must first be registered with Subsidia. Use the form at [Register new supplier](https://help.subsidia.ch/de/articles/5052712-neuer-lieferant-marke-bei-subsidia-erfassen-lassen).

---

## 2. Configure a brand

1. Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
2. Click **My Brands**.
3. Click **Configure Brand**.

![Brand list with configuration button](/images/en/marken-liste.png)

4. Search for the desired brand.
5. Click **Save**.

---

## 3. Link brand to supplier

The link defines which supplier delivers which brand. When creating articles, the system automatically selects the matching supplier when you choose the brand.

### Via the supplier view

1. Open **Settings > My Suppliers**.
2. Click the desired supplier.
3. Switch to the **Brands** tab.
4. Click **Add Brand**.
5. Select the brand and configure the **pricing factors** (revenue factor, minimum factor, maximum deviation).
6. Click **Save**.

![Supplier-brand linking](/images/en/lieferant-marken-verknuepfung.png)

### Automatic price calculation

When you configure pricing factors, the system automatically calculates the sales price based on the purchase price and the configured factor when creating an article.

---

## 4. Set delivery and payment terms

1. Open **Settings > My Suppliers**.
2. Click the desired supplier.
3. On the **Edit** tab, you find the fields for terms:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Delivery terms** | Type of delivery (dropdown). |
| **Terms of payment in days** | Number of days until payment is due. |
| **Discount** | Percentage discount for early payment (via **Add discount**). |

4. Click **Save**.

---

## 5. Order-only goods receipt

You can require that goods receipts for a supplier are only possible with an existing order.

1. Open **Settings > My Suppliers**.
2. Click the desired supplier.
3. Enable the **Order required for deliveries** checkbox.
4. Click **Save**.

> **Note:** When this option is active, the POS App only allows adding articles from existing orders to goods receipts.

---

## 6. Invite a contact

To send orders directly from the Admin App to a brand representative, invite the contact.

1. Open **Settings > My Brands**.
2. Click the desired brand.
3. Click **Edit**.
4. Enter the representative's **email address**.
5. Click **Invite via Email**.
6. Select the **language** for the invitation email.
7. Click **Send Invitation**.
8. Click **Save**.

> **Note:** The representative must accept the invitation before you can send orders to them.

---

## Remove a supplier

1. Open **Settings > My Suppliers**.
2. Click the desired supplier.
3. Click **Delete**.
4. Confirm the removal.

---

## Frequently asked questions

**My supplier is not displayed — what should I do?**
The supplier must be configured under **Settings > My Suppliers**. Only suppliers configured for your account appear in the filters.

**What does the brand-supplier link do?**
When you select a brand during article creation, the system automatically fills in the linked supplier. The configured price factor calculates the sales price from the purchase price.

**Can I override the price calculation manually?**
Yes. When creating or editing an article, you can always adjust the sales price manually.
