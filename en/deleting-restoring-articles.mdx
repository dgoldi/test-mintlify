You delete individual variants or entire articles, restore deleted variants, and clean up duplicate articles.

---

## Prerequisites

- Access to the **Admin App** with **Admin rights** (not possible with a Team Account)
- Permission for the **Articles** section

---

## 1. Delete variants

You can deactivate individual variants of an article without deleting the entire article.

1. Open the **Admin App**.
2. Click **Articles** in the sidebar.
3. Open the desired article.
4. Switch to the **Articles** tab.
5. Enable the **checkboxes** next to the variants you want to delete.
6. Click **Delete** in the action bar.
7. Confirm the deletion.

![Variant list with selection](/images/en/artikel-varianten-loeschen.png)

The deleted variants are deactivated and no longer visible in the system — they can be restored if needed.

> **Note:** Deleted variants remain in existing shipments and orders.

---

## 2. Restore variants

You can reactivate deleted variants at any time.

1. Open the article and switch to the **Articles** tab.
2. Enable the **Show inactive** checkbox.

The system shows the deactivated variants in the list.

![Variant list with inactive variants](/images/en/artikel-varianten-wiederherstellen.png)

3. **Single variant:** Click **Reactivate** at the end of the row.
4. **Multiple variants:** Enable the checkboxes and click **Reactivate** in the action bar.
5. Confirm the restoration.

---

## 3. Delete an entire article

To permanently delete an article with all its variants and barcodes:

1. Open the article.
2. Switch to the **Advanced** tab.
3. Click **Delete**.

![Delete article on the Advanced tab](/images/en/artikel-loeschen-erweitert.png)

4. Enter the number of variants to confirm the deletion.
5. Enable the checkbox **I want to delete all related Article-Barcodes** to avoid duplicate barcodes.
6. Click **Delete**.

> **Important:** This action cannot be undone. Orders and shipments with these variants remain untouched.

---

## 4. Clean up duplicate articles

If an article was accidentally created twice, clean up the duplicates in four steps:

1. **Find the duplicate** — Search for the duplicate articles in the article list.
2. **Choose the version** — Check which version should be kept.
3. **Transfer stock** — Remove the stock from the incorrect article and transfer it to the correct one.
4. **Deactivate the incorrect article** — Delete the variants of the incorrect article (see section 1) or delete the entire article (see section 3).

> **Note:** Stock adjustments are made via the Stocktaking App or the Admin App. Make sure the incorrect article has no remaining stock before deactivating it.

---

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I restore deleted variants?**
Yes. Enable the **Show inactive** checkbox on the **Articles** tab and click **Reactivate**.

**Can I restore an entire deleted article?**
No. Entire articles deleted via the **Advanced** tab cannot be restored. Only individual variants can be reactivated.

**What happens to the barcodes when deleting?**
Barcodes are only deleted if you enable the corresponding checkbox during deletion. Without this option, barcodes remain and can cause duplicates.
