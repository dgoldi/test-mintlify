You create new shifts with payment methods, receipt configuration, and assign them to a store.

---

## Prerequisites

- Access to the **Admin App** with **Admin rights**
- At least one **Store** set up in the system

---

## 1. Create a new shift

1. Open the **Admin App**.
2. Click **Settings** in the sidebar.
3. Click **Shifts**.
4. Click **Add Shift**.

![Shifts list with Add Shift button](/images/en/schichten-liste.png)

5. Fill in the following fields:

| Field | Required | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Name** | Yes | Internal name of the shift. |
| **Official Name** | Yes | Name on receipts and documents. |
| **Store** | Yes | Associated store. |
| **Email** | No | Contact email (printed on receipts). |
| **Phone** | No | Contact phone (printed on receipts). |

6. Click **Save**.

![Shift form with fields](/images/en/schicht-formular.png)

---

## 2. Configure payment methods

After creating the shift, configure the payment methods.

1. Open the shift.
2. Switch to the **Payment** tab.
3. Click **Add Payment Method**.
4. Select the **payment type** (e.g., cash, card).
5. Select the **currency**.
6. Define when the register should be counted (opening, closing, or reset to zero).
7. Click **Save**.

Repeat the steps for additional payment methods.

---

## 3. Configure shift reports

1. Open the shift.
2. Enable the **Shift report enabled** checkbox.
3. Select the **recipients** for automatic delivery.
4. Define whether the report should include **details**.
5. Click **Save**.

> **Note:** The report is sent automatically via email to the selected recipients when the shift is closed.

### Download a report manually

1. Open the shift.
2. Click **Shift Report** in the top right.
3. Select a **date range** (maximum 7 days).
4. Click **Download**.

---

## 4. Reprint counts

1. Open **Settings > Shifts**.
2. Click the desired shift.
3. Switch to the **Counts** tab.
4. Click the printer icon next to the desired count.

> **Note:** Count receipts cannot be sent via email. Reprinting is done directly at the terminal.

---

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I change payment methods later?**
Yes. Open the shift and edit the payment methods on the **Payment** tab.

**How do I change the selection duration on the receipt?**
Open the shift and adjust the **selection duration in days** and the **text**.
