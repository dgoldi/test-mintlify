## Prerequisites

- Access to the **Admin App** with **Admin rights**
- Permission for the **Gift Cards** section

---

## Block a gift card

1. Open the **Admin App**.
2. Click **Gift Cards** in the sidebar.
3. Search for the desired gift card.
4. Click the gift card.

![Gift cards list](/images/en/geschenkkarten-liste.png)

5. Click **Change Date** in the top right.
6. Enter a **past date**.
7. Click **Save**.

The gift card is blocked and can no longer be redeemed.

> **Important:** The blocking date cannot be earlier than the gift card's last transaction.

---

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I unblock a blocked gift card?**
Yes. Set the expiry date to a future date.
