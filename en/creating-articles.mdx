## Prerequisites

- Access to the **Admin App**
- Permission for the **Articles** section
- At least one **Brand**, one **Supplier**, and one **Category** exist in the system

---

## Overview

The process in six steps:

1. **Open the article form** — You navigate to the creation form.
2. **Fill in model details** — You enter brand, supplier, article number, and category.
3. **Assign season and collection** — You optionally assign the article to a season and collection.
4. **Set prices** — You define purchase price, sales price, and MSRP.
5. **Define variations** — You specify colors, sizes, and materials.
6. **Save the article** — The system creates the article with all variations.

For articles without variations, a simplified form is available — see the section [Creating a single article without variations](#creating-a-single-article-without-variations).

---

## 1. Open the article form

1. Open the **Admin App**.
2. Click **Articles** in the sidebar.
3. Click **Add Article** in the top right corner.

The system displays the creation form with the title **Article — create**.

![Article list with Add Article button](/images/en/artikel-liste.png)

> **Note:** If you want to carry over data from the last entry, click **Pre-fill from last entry**. The system fills all fields except the **Article Number** with the previously entered values. The button only appears if you have previously created an article.

---

## 2. Fill in model details

The form is divided into two columns: On the left, you enter the model details, season/collection, and prices. On the right, you define the variations (colors, sizes, material).

![Overview of the creation form](/images/en/artikel-formular.png)

Fill in the following fields in the **Model Details** section:

| Field | Required | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Brand** | Yes | Brand of the article. When you select a supplier, the system restricts the brand selection to that supplier's brands. |
| **Supplier** | Yes | Supplier of the article. When you select a brand, the system restricts the supplier selection accordingly. |
| **Article Number** | Yes | Name or number of the article. |
| **Article Description** | No | Optional description of the article. |
| **Category** | Yes | Category of the article. The system shows recommendations based on supplier and size system. |
| **Assortment Group** | No | Optional assignment to an assortment group. |
| **Tags** | No | Optional tags for labeling. |

![Model details with filled fields](/images/en/artikel-angaben.png)

> **Note:** **Brand** and **Supplier** filter each other. If only one matching entry exists, the system selects it automatically.

---

## 3. Assign season and collection

In the **Season & Collection** section, you can optionally assign the article to a season and collection:

1. Select a **Season** if needed.
2. Select a **Collection** if needed. The system shows recommendations based on the selected supplier.

Both fields are optional. If you do not need an assignment, skip this section.

![Assigning season and collection](/images/en/artikel-saison.png)

---

## 4. Set prices

Fill in the following fields in the **Price** section:

| Field | Required | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Purchase Price** | Yes | Purchase price of the article. |
| **Sales Price** | Yes | Sales price for the end customer. |

### Setting the MSRP

The checkbox **MSRP equals Sales Price** is enabled by default. The system uses the sales price as the manufacturer's suggested retail price (MSRP).

If the MSRP differs from the sales price:

1. Disable the checkbox **MSRP equals Sales Price**.
2. Enter the desired **MSRP**.

### Setting the original price

If the sales price differs from an original price (e.g., for promotions):

1. Disable the checkbox **Sales Price equals Original Price**.
2. Enter the **Original Price**.

The system calculates the **Discount** automatically as a percentage.

![Prices with purchase and sales price](/images/en/artikel-preise.png)

> **Note:** When you select a supplier, the system automatically uses that supplier's stored currency for the purchase price.

---

## 5. Define variations

On the right side of the form, you define the article's variations. The system automatically creates all combinations of the specified colors, sizes, and materials.

### Color Variations

In the **Color Variations** section, enter the available colors. Confirm each entry individually. This field is optional — without a color entry, the system does not create color variations.

### Size Dimension

In the **Size Dimension** section, select a size system and the corresponding sizes. The system shows recommendations based on the selected supplier and category.

> **Important:** The **Size Dimension** is a required field. Without a size specification, the article cannot be saved.

### Material Variations

In the **Material Variations** section, enter the material composition (e.g., "80% Cotton, 20% Polyester"). Confirm each entry individually. This field is optional.

![Variation fields for colors, sizes, and material](/images/en/artikel-varianten.png)

---

## 6. Save the article

1. Click **Save**.
2. The system creates the article with all variation combinations and redirects you to the article overview.

On the article overview, the following tabs are available: **Overview**, **Edit**, **Articles**, **Orders**, **Open Positions**, **Shipments**, **Batches**, and **Advanced**.

![Article overview after successful creation](/images/en/artikel-uebersicht.png)

> **Note:** On the **Articles** tab, you can add further variations afterwards — using the **Add Variations** button with the options **Add Color Set**, **Add Material Set**, or **Add Size Set**.

## Creating a single article without variations

For articles without color, size, or material variations, a simplified form is available.

1. Open the article form as described in step 1.
2. Click **Add Single Article** in the top right corner.
3. Fill in the **Model Details** (same fields as in the standard form, without season and collection).
4. Set the **Prices** (same fields as in the standard form).
5. Click **Save**.

### Differences from the standard form

| Property | Standard creation | Single article |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Color Variations | Yes | No |
| Size Dimension | Yes (required) | No |
| Material Variations | Yes | No |
| Season & Collection | Yes | No |
| Result | Article with variations | Single article without variations |

## Frequently asked questions

**How many variations does the system create?** The system creates one variation for each combination of color, size, and material. Example: 3 colors, 4 sizes, and 2 materials result in 24 variations.

**Can I add variations afterwards?** Yes. Open the article and switch to the **Articles** tab. Using **Add Variations**, you can add further colors, sizes, or materials.

**Can I convert a single article into a variation article afterwards?** Yes. Open the article, switch to the **Articles** tab, and add colors, sizes, or materials there.
