Here you learn how to scan a new customer's registration code in the POS or Office and create the customer record.

> If you have not yet set up the QR code for the registration form, read the guide [Setting Up QR Code Customer Registration](digital-customer-registration.md) first.

---

## Prerequisites

- Access to the **POS** or **Office**
- The customer has completed the registration form and received a **registration code**

---

## Creating a customer via customer registration

1. In the **POS** or **Office**, open customer registration under **Customers > Create Customer**.
2. Scan the customer's **registration code** using the search bar or barcode scanner.
3. The system automatically transfers the customer data into the form.

![Customer form with pre-filled data](/images/en/pos-kundenerfassung.png)

4. Review or complete the details as needed.
5. Click **Save**.

---

## Creating a customer directly in a transaction

You can also scan the registration code during an ongoing sale:

1. Scan the registration code during an ongoing **transaction**.
2. The system automatically opens the customer registration form with the pre-filled data.
3. Review or complete the details.
4. Click **Save**.

The system assigns the new customer directly to the transaction.

---

## Duplicate check

When the new customer's email address already exists in the system, the system automatically displays an overview of matching customers. You then have two options:

- **Select an existing customer** — The system links the existing customer to the transaction.
- **Register new customer** — Click **Register new customer** to create a new entry regardless.

---

## Frequently asked questions

**Which apps support scanning the registration code?**
The **POS** and the **Office** can process the registration code. In the **Admin App**, you only configure the setup.
