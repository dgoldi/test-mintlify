You set minimum stock levels per article and store and generate order proposals automatically to replenish inventory.

---

## Prerequisites

- Access to the **Admin App**
- Permission for the **Minimum Stock** section
- **L-Package** is activated (NOS/Reass feature)

---

## 1. Set minimum stock

1. Open the **Admin App**.
2. Click **Minimum Stock** in the sidebar.
3. Select the desired article.
4. Click **Set Minimum Stock**.

![Minimum stock list](/images/en/mindestbestand-liste.png)

5. Select the **stores** for which the minimum stock should apply.
6. Click **Add to Minimum Stock**.
7. Enter the **minimum quantities** per store.

The minimum stock is saved.

---

## 2. Generate order proposal

1. Open the **Minimum Stock** section.
2. Click **Generate Order**.
3. Select the **store** and **supplier**.
4. Click **Show Preview** to review the proposal.
5. Click **Generate Orders**.

The system automatically creates orders based on the differences between current stock and minimum stock levels.

---

## Frequently asked questions

**Which packages include this feature?**
The NOS/Reass feature is included in the L-Package.

**Can I set minimum stock for multiple stores at once?**
Yes. When setting the minimum stock, you can select multiple stores simultaneously.
