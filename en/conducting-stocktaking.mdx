You create a stocktaking, start the count, review the differences, and confirm the stocktaking. The system posts the stock corrections automatically.

---

## Prerequisites

- Access to the **Admin App**
- Permission for the **Stocktakings** section
- All open **goods receipts** and **delivery notes** for the affected store are completed or cancelled

---

## Overview

The process in four steps:

1. **Prepare the stocktaking** — You create a new stocktaking and select the store.
2. **Start the stocktaking** — You start the count. Participants can join via a shared link.
3. **Review differences** — You check the deviations between expected and actual stock and adjust quantities manually if needed.
4. **Confirm the stocktaking** — You confirm the stocktaking. The system posts the differences.

---

## 1. Prepare the stocktaking

Before the stocktaking, all open stock movements must be completed:

- Complete all **goods receipts** with the status "In Progress".
- Cancel or complete all prepared **delivery notes**.

Then create the stocktaking:

1. Open the **Admin App**.
2. Click **Stocktakings** in the sidebar.
3. Click **Prepare Stocktaking** in the top right.

![Stocktakings list with Prepare Stocktaking button](/images/en/inventuren-liste.png)

4. Select the **Store**.
5. The **Start Stocktaking now** checkbox is enabled by default. Disable it to set a later date.
6. Click **Save**.

![Stocktaking form with store and start options](/images/en/inventur-formular.png)

The system creates the stocktaking and redirects you to the overview.

> **Important:** Once the stocktaking starts, no goods receipts can be processed. All articles sold during the stocktaking must be counted.

---

## 2. Start the stocktaking

If the stocktaking was not started immediately (checkbox disabled), start it manually:

1. Open the stocktaking.
2. Switch to the **Advanced** tab.
3. Click **Start now**.

![Start stocktaking on the Advanced tab](/images/en/inventur-starten.png)

The system sets the status to **In Progress**.

### Invite participants

On the **Overview** tab, you find a link that you can send to participants. Participants use the Stocktaking App to scan articles and submit their counts.

![Stocktaking overview with statistics and participation link](/images/en/inventur-uebersicht.png)

The overview shows the current progress:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Scanned** | Number of articles scanned so far |
| **Should** | Expected stock according to the system |
| **Manually recorded** | Manually adjusted quantities |
| **Difference** | Deviation between expected and actual stock |

---

## 3. Review differences

Once participants have submitted their counts, review the deviations.

1. Open the stocktaking.
2. Switch to the **Differences** tab.

![Differences tab with article list](/images/en/inventur-differenzen.png)

The list shows all articles with the columns **Expected**, **Actual**, **Rating**, **Diff. %**, and **Diff.**.

### Filter

Use the filter options to search for specific deviations:

- **Understock** — fewer counted than expected
- **Overstock** — more counted than expected
- **Equalized** — expected and actual stock match

### Adjust quantities manually

1. Click a row in the differences list.
2. The system shows the individual article variants (color, size, material) with their quantities.
3. Adjust the **actual quantity** and the system saves the change automatically.

![Article detail with variants and quantity adjustment](/images/en/inventur-artikel-detail.png)

> **Note:** Manual adjustments are only possible when the status is **In Progress**.

---

## 4. Confirm the stocktaking

After all differences have been reviewed and all scan processes are completed:

1. Switch to the **Advanced** tab.
2. Click **Confirm now**.

![Confirm stocktaking on the Advanced tab](/images/en/inventur-abschliessen.png)

3. Confirm in the dialog.

The system sets the status to **Confirmed** and posts the differences. A confirmed stocktaking no longer allows changes.

> **Important:** The **Confirm now** button only appears when all participant scan processes are completed.

---

## Cancel or annul a stocktaking

| Action | Available in status | Effect |
|--------|---------------------|--------|
| **Cancel now** | New, In Progress | Counts are ignored. No differences posted. |
| **Annul now** | Confirmed | Posted differences are reverted. |

Both actions are available on the **Advanced** tab.

> **Important:** Cancelling and annulling cannot be undone.

---

## Frequently asked questions

**Why does the "Confirm now" button not appear?**
All participant scan processes must be completed. Check the **Participants** tab for any open scan processes.

**Can I correct differences after confirmation?**
No. After confirmation, no changes are possible. You can annul the stocktaking and start a new one.

**Can goods receipts be processed during a stocktaking?**
No. Goods receipts are blocked from the start of the stocktaking until it is confirmed or cancelled.
